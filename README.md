Walmart Retail Analytics & Demand Forecasting

End-to-End Retail Intelligence Project



Project Overview

Retail businesses depend heavily on accurate demand forecasting, inventory optimization, and sales performance analysis. Small forecasting errors can lead to overstocking, stockouts, and revenue loss.

This project analyzes Walmart retail data to understand sales trends, forecast demand patterns, and generate business intelligence insights that support operational and strategic decision-making.

The goal is to simulate a real-world retail analytics system used by business and supply chain teams.



Business Problem

Retail companies face several challenges:

* Unpredictable demand fluctuations
* Inefficient inventory planning
* Seasonal sales variation
* Lack of forecasting systems
* Limited visibility into product performance

This project addresses these issues using data-driven forecasting and analytics.



Objectives

* Analyze historical sales trends
* Identify seasonal demand patterns
* Forecast future sales demand
* Evaluate product and store performance
* Improve inventory planning decisions



Dataset

Retail sales data including:

* Weekly sales
* Store information
* Product categories
* Holiday indicators
* Time-based features



Tools Used

Python | SQL | Excel | Power BI | Tableau

Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn



Methodology

* Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
* Time Series Analysis
* Feature Engineering (seasonality, holidays)
* Demand Forecasting
* KPI Development
* Dashboard Creation



Key Insights

* Sales fluctuate significantly during holiday periods
* Certain stores consistently outperform others
* Seasonal trends strongly influence demand
* Historical patterns can be used for accurate forecasting



Business Impact

* Improved inventory planning
* Reduced stockouts and overstocking
* Better demand prediction accuracy
* Increased operational efficiency
* Data-driven retail decision-making



Outcome

This project demonstrates how retail organizations can use analytics and forecasting to improve supply chain efficiency and revenue performance.
